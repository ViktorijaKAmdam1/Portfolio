About Me

Frontend Developer

I grew up in Lithuania, in a family of six - as the oldest child. There I got great organisation skills. My grandparents were teachers - so from early chilhood I learned a thing, which became my motto: DO IT PERFECT, OR DON'T DO ANYTHING AT ALL. In simple words - it's a responsibility. I'm so lucky that because of amazing people around me, I had a chance to grow a great set of values such as DUTIFULNESS, RESPECT and a huge will to LEARN new things.

I have to admit, that my Grandmothers amazing taste for estetics in life and things, became as a great inspiration for my own life - from my first steps, it's a huge part of myself as well.

In my life I have accumulated quite big amount of expierence and knowledge - earned a bachelor degree in Agronomy, have studied plant biotechnology and was quite active student union member. A lot of expierence with customer service enriched my personality and gave me great comunication skills.

Some interesting facts about me:

  • I was left in the 10th grade for a second year. After that I came back to normal school, and even finished bachelor studies and defended the dissertation for 9/10.
  • I love nature, and have a understanding based on education - how everything works. I would love to join projects related to science (bioscience, ecology, klimat change and etc.)
  • I love handcrafts - to sew is one of my passions.
